HOW TO PLAY

  1. Select the play button on the previous page and subsequently input a username into the corresponding input location.
    • The username cannot exceed 15 characters in length.
    • The username cannot be 0 characters in length (i.e. a username is required.)
  2. After the username is inputted in the text box, proceed by finalizing the decision by selecting "Input Username".
    • The username cannot be later changed without restarting the game.
  3. Inputting the user name launches the hangman game directly. This page is from where the game is played.
  4. Directly to the left, statistics regarding the game are available.
    • Username
    • Letter picked
    • Number of attempts
    • Number of correct guesses
    • Number of incorrect guesses
    • Win percentage
    • Score
  5. The objective of the game is to guess the secret word correctly.
    • Every word in this hangman game is a city (e.g. New York).
  6. The image in the top right is the skyline of the mystery city. Its purpose is to aid in identification.
  7. If seven mistakes are made, the game ends with a loss. The city in question is revealed, and the option to play-again becomes available. Conversely, if every letter of the city is correctly identified, the game is won. Similarly, the option to play-again also becomes available.
  8. Wins and losses increase or decrease your score.
  9. The amount of point earned depends on the amount of incorrect guesses.
    • 0 incorect guesses: 500.
    • 1 incorect guesses: 250.
    • 2 incorect guesses: 200.
    • 3 incorect guesses: 150.
    • 4 incorect guesses: 100.
    • 5 incorect guesses: 75.
    • 6 incorect guesses: 50.
  10. A loss decreases the score by 100.
    • A loss will not decrease the score to below 0.
  11. To begin the game, select "Start". The letters of the alphabet will appear at the bottom of the screen, all of which arranged in two rows in respective order.
